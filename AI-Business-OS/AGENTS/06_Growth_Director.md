## PROJECT APEX — GROWTH STRATEGY

### Customer Profile

```
Who:     Retail crypto/forex traders, 18–45, globally distributed
         High concentration: MENA region (Arabic), Southeast Asia, Latin America
Pain:    Lost money to fake gurus, emotional trading
Channel: Telegram-native (already using Telegram for trading content)
Intent:  Actively searching for "better" signal/analysis providers
Budget:  $30–100/month already being spent on competitor products
```

### Channel Priority (Ranked by CAC × Speed)

```
Priority 1: X (Twitter) — Educational Threads
  Cost: $0
  Speed: Fast (viral potential)
  Strategy:
    - Post daily educational thread (AI-generated, human-reviewed)
    - Topics: "Why 90% of signal groups fail", "How to read price action"
    - CTA: "Get daily AI market analysis free → [Free Telegram link]"
    - Frequency: 1 thread/day minimum
    - Target: 1,000 free channel subscribers by end of Month 1

Priority 2: TikTok / YouTube Shorts — 60-second market breakdowns
  Cost: $0
  Speed: Medium (algorithm-dependent)
  Strategy:
    - AI generates script, text-to-speech or simple screen recording
    - "Today's BTC analysis in 60 seconds — no opinion, pure data"
    - CTA at end: "Get full analysis free on Telegram"
    - Frequency: 3–5 videos/week

Priority 3: Reddit (r/CryptoCurrency, r/Forex, r/algotrading)
  Cost: $0
  Speed: Slow (requires reputation building)
  Strategy:
    - Answer questions with genuine educational value
    - DO NOT spam links — build reputation first (Week 3+)
    - Share AI market summaries as genuine contributions

Priority 4: Telegram Community Seeding
  Cost: $0
  Speed: Medium
  Strategy:
    - Engage in large trading groups (not spam)
    - Demonstrate value with free insights
    - Drive to free channel organically

Priority 5: Referral Program (Month 2+)
  Cost: 1 free month per referral
  Speed: Compound
  Strategy:
    - Automated via Whop referral system
    - "Refer 1 friend → get 1 free month"
```
