## PROJECT APEX — SALES STRATEGY

### Primary Sales Mechanism

Since this is a B2C subscription ($49/mo), sales are automated via a "Product-Led Sales" model. The "Free Channel" acts as the automated sales agent.

**Value Communication:**
Show, don't tell. Post actual AI analysis in the free channel.
When a market move happens that our VIP analysis predicted/identified, post a recap:
*"VIP members got this level yesterday. Do you want to stop guessing? Join VIP."*

**Handling Objections (Automated via Content):**
- Objection: "Is this just another scam group?"
- Answer (Content): "We don't sell guarantees. We sell emotionless AI data."
- Objection: "Is it worth $49?"
- Answer (Content): "1 bad emotional trade costs you $500. We cost $49 to prevent it."

### Secondary Sales Mechanism (DMs)

If users DM the bot asking questions before buying:
- Bot sends FAQ automated responses.
- If user needs human touch, flag for human owner. (MVP will not build complex conversational sales bot to avoid hallucination risk).
