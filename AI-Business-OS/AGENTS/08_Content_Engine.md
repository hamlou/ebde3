## PROJECT APEX — CONTENT STRATEGY

### Core Philosophy
"Educate, don't hype." We are positioning ourselves against the "get rich quick" scammers. Our content must look and feel analytical, objective, and slightly boring (which builds trust in a noisy market).

### Content Pillars
1. **Market Reality:** Exposing how the market actually works (liquidity grabs, slippage).
2. **Algorithmic Trading Concepts:** Explaining complex concepts simply (RSI divergence, order blocks).
3. **Psychology & Risk:** The math behind position sizing. "Why 90% fail."
4. **Daily Data Drops:** Snippets of our actual VIP AI analysis (Top of Funnel).

### 30-Day Content Library Pipeline (Pre-Launch Requirement)
We will use an LLM script to generate a 30-day backlog of content to post so the channel doesn't look empty.

**Example Prompt for Generation:**
"Generate an educational post for Telegram about position sizing. Use clear formatting, bullet points, and an objective tone. Do not use emojis. Explain the 1% risk rule."
